# SIGILFAE TESTIS

> Witness fairy. Token: `w`. Member of **THE :VARIETY** (smart-contract clade).

A neutral third-party fairy with a small notepad on her chest and a pencil
that writes by itself in the air next to her. TESTIS observes a `:` contract
without participating in either side — her presence makes the deal auditable.

## Behaviour

- Spawns when `w` appears inside a `:` contract.
- Layout: positioned to the LEFT of the empire/user bond, off-axis, like a
  scribe taking notes from a distance.
- Earns a small **white blood fee** for being there (paid out of the empire's
  share when the contract honours).
- Click → tags her notepad as "WITNESSED" (seals her observation).
- On contract honour → her notes are immutably attached to the deal-receipt.
- On contract collapse → her notes record the default for future reference.

## DOTGRAMMA usage (always inside `:`)

```
:w-b.0020         → witness-observed biscuit deal, 20s countdown
:w-e-s-Q.0060     → witness + escrow + soul + base, 60s
:w-w-b.0025       → multiple witnesses (extra trust, multiple notes)
```

## Why "Testis"?

Latin for **witness / testimony**. (Yes, also the modern English homophone —
the empire wears it on the chin.) Etymologically aligned with the binomial
naming convention.
