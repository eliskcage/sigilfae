# SIGILFAE FISCUS

> Escrow pot fairy. Token: `e`. Member of **THE :VARIETY** (smart-contract clade).

A small fairy carrying a literal **escrow pot** on her chest — a brown ceramic
vessel with a `$` jangling inside (animated tilt). FISCUS sits ON the bond
between empire and user, holding both sides' funds visibly until the contract
resolves.

## Behaviour

- Spawns when `e` appears inside a `:` contract.
- Layout: positioned at the **midpoint of the empire/user bond** — the literal
  middle-ground holding the funds.
- Multiple `e` tokens stack horizontally near the bond midpoint.
- On contract honour → pot dissolves into gold particles · funds release to
  the receiving side.
- On contract collapse → pot dissolves into dark particles · funds refund to
  the side that committed them. **No human admin involved.**
- Pot's `$` symbol jangles softly while pending (visible "money in motion").

## Why "Fiscus"?

Latin for **treasury / public purse / strongbox**. The empire's word for the
neutral middle that holds value in trust. Etymologically aligned with NOTAE,
PYXIS, CRUX, ANIMA, PANIS, OPERA, APIS, TESSERA, QUADRA, TESTIS.
